Caroling For Cans 2014

Caroling For Cans is an event that coincides with the Winter Food Drive.

It was a spontaneous decision by Amanda, Jingwen, Grace, Alexis, and Kate, 5 council members, to go caroling around the neighbourhood on a chilly evening. Unfortunately, the adventure was short-lived as it was over in around 45 minutes.
