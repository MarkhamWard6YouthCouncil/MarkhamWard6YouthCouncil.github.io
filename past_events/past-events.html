Past Events

Caroling for Cans

Caroling For Cans is an annual event that began in December 2014 with just 5 of our council members. The purpose of this event is to collect donations and encourage local residents to donate canned foods to the Markham Food Bank. Short jingles were created to the tune of popular Christmas songs to keep everything fun and simple for anyone to participate! This activity coincides with our Winter Food Drive.

Winter Food Drive

The Winter Food Drive holds the legacy as the council's first event. Considering that the council has been officially established in October, around 2 months prior the Winter Food Drive, it was an ambitious feat to accomplish the food drive. It took a lot of time and coordination to get the campaign rolling at first, but the council members were determined. They set up collection bins for canned foods in four public Markham locations, and promoted the food drive to fellow students and on social media.

Capture the Flag

On Wednesday, June 24th, 2015, we held an exciting game of Capture The Flag at Berczy Park. It was officially the last day of school for high school students, and we wanted to end it off in a high note!
